Palm Beach County has 58+ Forbes billionaires and a combined top-resident net worth of ~$495B. The median county income ($84k) masks extreme wealth concentration at the top. One of the 3 wealthiest counties in the United States.

The addressable YouTube Shorts audience of qualified prospects (55+, $2.5M+) is roughly 15,000–20,000 people. At $8–18 CPM, you could reach every one of them for $150–360/month. That is the cheapest qualified lead pool in high-net-worth financial marketing.

Competition
Tier 1 — Entrenched (hardest to displace)
Raymond James, Merrill Lynch, UBS, Morgan Stanley

Manage most of the $1–10M bracket. Clients are largely complacent. Advisors are salespeople, not thesis investors. Zero ability to articulate a specific AI infrastructure view. Their weakness: they never explain why.

Tier 2 — Boutique RIAs (direct competition)
Dozens of Palm Beach Gardens / Boca Raton boutiques

Small RIAs managing $50M–$500M each, many relocated from CT/NY post-2020. Targeting the same $2–8M investor pool. More sophisticated than the wire houses but still generalist. Can't match a specific documented track record.

Tier 3 — Big Names (aspirational, not direct)
Elliott Management (West Palm), Appaloosa, Citadel

These have moved to South Florida. $5–25M minimums. They've set the benchmark for what "serious money" looks like locally but don't compete for the same LP pool Dean is targeting initially.

Dean's Competitive Moat

Prospect Profiles — In Order of Priority
#1 Best Prospect
The Post-COVID Finance Migrant
Age 42–58 $5–25M liquid Palm Beach Gardens / Jupiter ZIP: 33418, 33458

Former hedge fund, PE, or finance exec who left NYC/Greenwich for Florida 2020–2023. Their brain never left the game. They intimately understand fund structures, AI, and alpha generation — but their current advisor is too conservative and boring. They are actively looking for what Dean has.

  • Pain point: Left the industry but miss the action. Current wealth manager puts them in 60/40. They hate it.
  • Why they join: Recognize the AI infrastructure thesis immediately. Have never seen someone their age with this specific a track record doing something this well-documented.
  • Social proof needed: The thesis document. The track record. A 30-minute conversation with Dean.
#2 Highest Volume
The Boca Business-Exit Crowd
Age 55–70 $3–12M liquid Boca Raton ZIP: 33431, 33433, 33496, 33498

Self-made business owner who sold their company 5–10 years ago. Strong concentration in Boca's Jewish community — exceptionally financially sophisticated, DIY research culture. Fundamentally skeptical of generic advisor advice. Does their own reading.

  • Pain point: Advisors put them in index funds. They want something differentiated that they understand and actually believe in.
  • Why they join: Research-first culture resonates deeply. Dean explains WHY in 18,000 words. No other manager in their life has ever done this.
  • Highest reach: This is the largest sub-market for the campaign. Boca alone has more qualified prospects than all of Wellington.
#3 Highest Trust Conversion
The Wellington Community
Age 48–65 $2–8M liquid Wellington ZIP: 33414

Dean's literal neighbors. Equestrian community — tight-knit, trust-based, and deeply connected. One Wellington investor typically generates 5 organic introductions. The referral network effect here is unmatched.

  • Best for: First 5–10 LPs. Community closes are fast when trust is established.
  • Event strategy: A private dinner with 10 Wellington community members = potential for 3–5 LPs and 20 referrals.
#4 High Value / Harder to Reach
Palm Beach Island — Old & New Money
Age 55–75 $10M+ Palm Beach Island ZIP: 33480

Ads alone won't work here. These people operate through networks and warm introductions. The path: one Wellington or Boca LP introduces Dean to someone on the island. The "neighbor" credibility bridges the gap.

#5 Good Volume
South Florida Medical Professionals
Age 55–68 $2–5M liquid Boca / Boynton / Delray

Doctors and specialists who've accumulated wealth over careers. Understand AI because medicine is transforming from it — they're seeing it firsthand in their practices. Respond to research-backed, well-documented content.

The Asymmetry Nobody Talks About

Google knows who has money. Not from what they say — from what they do: purchase history, travel behavior, real estate search activity, luxury brand engagement, financial news consumption patterns.

When you layer the zip code + age + interest stack above, you're not reaching all 1.58M people in Palm Beach County. You're reaching the 12,000–20,000 who fit the exact profile.

At typical YouTube Shorts CPMs for this targeting ($8–18 per 1,000 views), you could reach every single one of them for $150–360 per month. That is the cheapest qualified lead pool in high-net-worth financial marketing. Any single LP from this pool is worth more than a year's ad spend.

Seasonal Strategy

Palm Beach County has a defined "season" — snowbirds arrive October/November and most leave by May 1. You are currently at the tail end of season.

  • Now through May: Target full-time residents and late-departing snowbirds. Lower spend, test all 10 ad variants, identify top 3 performers.
  • June–September: Maintain minimal spend. Optimize. Build Dean's content library. Set up event infrastructure.
  • October launch: Full campaign. Snowbirds arrive fresh, wallets open, looking for new ideas for the new "season." This is when you scale.
